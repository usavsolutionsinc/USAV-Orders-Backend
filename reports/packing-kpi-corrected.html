How to read this

Weighted time = (Small × 5) + (Medium × 14) + (Large × 45) + (Unknown × 14), shown as hours & minutes.

% of day = weighted time ÷ 7h 30m (450 min packer shift). Over 100% means packing load exceeded a full shift.
