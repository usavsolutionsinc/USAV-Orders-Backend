## Design Context

### Users
Internal FBA operators and warehouse staff using the print queue during label prep, shipment tracking, and quick quantity edits.

### Brand Personality
Utilitarian, direct, calm. The interface should feel fast, exact, and low-friction.

### Aesthetic Direction
Brutally simple and icon-first. Prefer flat rows, minimal borders, and plain text over chips, bubbles, and decorative containers. Status should be communicated with compact icons and restrained color.

### Design Principles
- Keep the print queue readable at a glance.
- Use icons for status and quantity signals instead of decorative badges.
- Remove unnecessary framing, labels, and copy when the meaning is already obvious.
- Preserve all actions, but make them visually quiet.
- Optimize for speed of scanning and editing, not visual richness.
